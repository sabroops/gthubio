About me

Hello! I'm Sabrina Carlos-- I graduated from the University of California, Riverside in March 2022.

Throughout college, I was active in ACM/Women in Computing's mentorship program which gave me academic and professional guidance. I have also assisted in UCR's CS research (ML), where I learned from a large codebase and trained datasets using python scripts. Additionally, I was a grader for lower div CS labs. Lastly, this past year I was Chairperson for UCR's Filipino Student Organization (Katipunan PSO) , where I learned about my connection with my culture, how to work with others, and my leadership style.
